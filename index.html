Aki's Hairsalon

About

Aki is a creative stylist who passion for hair began in 2004 in Japan, where she has both trained and taught styling.

She came to Toronto to expand her repertoire of styles and to bring her own creative style to Canada. After a few years of working in a salon in Toronto, she ventured out on her own and started her own business. She prides herself on providing a specialized cut for each of her clients and cares immensely about details and texture, and it shows! Her focus is on having all her customers to look their best. She thanks all her past clients for their business and is always open to receiving new ones.
